# The Umamusume Racing Society (URS) Community Supplemental Rules for Community Spaces

*Last Edited: March 23, 2026*

The Umamusume Racing Society (URS) Community Supplemental Rules are a set of additional implementing rules in addition to the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) that will guide members of the community and staff alike to acceptable behavior in Discord, VRChat and related spaces the organisation controls.

## Additional Rules for Community Spaces

In addition to acceptable behavior set to standard by the [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md), the following behaviors is not acceptable:

* Violation of [Cygames' content guidelines for Uma Musume](https://umamusume.com/fan-createdguide/) in any way, shape or form.
   - This include(s) but not limited to the following:
     - Works made with the intent to injure the reputation of this franchise or other third parties.
     - Works of a violent, sexual, or otherwise obscene nature.
     - Works that advocate or criticize political views, religion, or ideology, or the use of derivative works for these purposes.
     - Works that involve illegal activities or other inappropriate conduct.
     - Works that infringe upon the rights of a third party.
     - Works or activities intended for commercial use or profit (i.e., commercial activity that exceeds the scope of fan-related activities)
     - Works or the use of derivative works that might otherwise be deemed inappropriate when taken in the context of this list

* Posting of Restricted Content
  - Any content that is/contains extreme violence, sexual content (either graphic or implied), or self-harm is strictly prohibited. Any restricted content that is sent to individuals without their consent is also grounds for moderation. We will not tolerate adults who send restricted content to minors with or without their consent.

* Alting and Impersonation of URS Staff
  - Malicious usage of alternate accounts in order to mislead staff, evade punishment, bypass the URS Community Conduct and its supplemental rules, or intentionally harass other members will be subject to any punishment as determined by URS Staff.
